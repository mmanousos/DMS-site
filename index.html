What is the Devil Mask Society?

We are a group of kinbaku enthusiasts focused on education for advancement of the art. We prioritize information based on primary source material and first-hand instruction.

The group was started in 2012 in Los Angeles, CA, but we now have members and friends spanning the globe.

As a group of friends who share common interests, we are an anarchist rope collective with no formal leaders.

DMS has grown exponentially through our local class structure (the various Cells) and public introductory classes.

Our members attend and teach at national and international conferences and various local rope groups. See what we're doing locally and where we'll be next on our Calendar or check out our list of Instructors to discover who is available to teach you.

If you are not local to southern California but are interested in our iterative teaching style, check out some other groups who have adopted our methods and curriculum.

The group can be found on Fetlife.

Or through our public account on Instagram.

We also have a Discord server for general conversations. We hold monthly Steering Committe meetings there every second Sunday at noon Pacific. All are welcome to the Steering Committee meetings - learn about what we're working on and get involved. Attendees have equal voice there to suggest or vote on topics.
