What is the Devil Mask Society?

We are a group of kinbaku enthusiasts focused on education for advancement of the art. We prioritize information based on primary source material and first-hand instruction.

The group was started in 2012 in Los Angeles, CA, but we now have members and friends spanning the globe.

As a group of friends who share common interests, we are an anarchist rope collective with no formal leaders.

DMS has grown exponentially through our local class structure (the various Cells) and public events like the Devil Mask Society Rope Jam.

Our members attend and teach at national and international conferences and various local rope groups. See what we're doing locally and where we'll be next on our Calendar.

In January 2024, three of our members - Ojipan, Ordinals, and goodmosttimes - launched Devil Mask Studio. This is a dedicated shibari studio in the heart of historic Downtown Los Angeles - a space for rope education and community. Learn more at devilmaskstudio.com.

With the launch of the Studio and after 10 years of teaching the curriculum, Ojipan and goodmosttimes have decided to phase out the tying Cell program and replace it with the Studio Scholarship program (Scholarship program details forthcoming). 11th Cell is the final tying Cell cohort. All of the tying Cell program materials - including recordings from the 11th Cell livestreamed lessons - remain available online for free. The bottoming curriculum, Cell X, will continue to be offered periodically. Learn more about the Cell X program.

The group can be found on Fetlife.

Or through our public account on Instagram.

We also have a Discord server for general conversations. We hold Steering Committe meetings there quarterly at noon Pacific on the second Sunday of January, April, July, and October. All are welcome to the Steering Committee meetings - learn about what we're working on and get involved. Attendees have equal voice there to suggest or vote on topics.
