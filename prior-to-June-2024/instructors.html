ñYADOM

ñyaDom became fascinated with rope and knots as a Boy Scout. This propelled his desire to learn Kinbaku.... that and... well you know! ;)
He joined DMS as a student in the 4th Cell cohort, graduating in 2016. He has since given back to the community as a TA for the subsequent DMS cohorts and co-host for the DMS monthly rope Social and Introductory Classes. He is presently hosting those two monthly events. He has also co-hosted the rope taster booths at Threshold Clubhouse in Los Angeles. He has continued his rope education by attending local weekend intensives with Nawashi Kanna, Pedro, Wykd Dave, Osada Steve, Kazami Ranki, Otonawa and others. 2018 saw him journey outside the LA rope scene attending Bondage Expo Dallas and Folsom Street Fair.

OJIPAN and GOODMOSTTIMES

The primary instructors behind the current Devil Mask Society Cell classes, Ojipan and goodmosttimes generally present together but can teach separately as requested. As a teaching team of switches, they specialize in addressing topics from both sides of a tie. Classes generally fall in the beginner to intermediate skill level range. They tailor the curriculum to the specifications of the host. They have travelled to teach intensives and at conventions throughout the US and to Canada. View their class list.

PAPERD0LL

Paperd0ll is a graduate of Devil Mask Society’s 6th Cell Cohort where she proudly earned the nickname, “Adult Panda.” She first entered the fetish scene in the mid-90’s when she frequented goth and industrial dance clubs and spent most of her time (and money) going to Brit-pop concerts. She began dabbling in rope in mid-2014, and strives to continually improve. She is a graduate of National Holistic Institute Los Angeles, and often incorporates her anatomy and massage training into her ropework. Although she is an avid rope enthusiast, she's a busy & multidimensional girl with many interests that she never has enough hours in the day to explore.
