ALTBRONTE

AltBronte has been doing rope in some fashion since 2012. She tops, bottoms, and self-suspends, and is happy to talk or teach about any of those subjects. Her style is semi-sadistic, playful, and constantly evolving. She offers classes about non-TK chest harnesses, tying as a smaller top, rope handling for short people, self-suspension, and intermediate bondage for sex, and is always developing new things to teach.

BADLITTLEDOE

badlittledoe has been participating in the kink community since 2011. Having served as both a sub and Dom, his insight on both perspectives helps provide new approaches to the conversation between a top and a bottom. He’s a graduate of Devil Mask Society’s 6th Cell, a 20 month shibari course. Seeing a void in the rope community in their area of Long Beach, CA, he and rhapsodic launched TIEHAUS - a place for foundational rope instruction with an emphasis on safety, negotiation, and consent. While he enjoys practicing shibari as an art form, he is meticulous about anatomy and its impact on rope play. He practices regularly, testing out ideas he finds online while musing his own concepts.

DANCERGODDESS

DancerGoddess began her formal rope education in 2015. She has been tying, self-tying, bottoming, and flying in rope since then. She is a graduate of the Devil Mask Society’s 8th Cell cohort. She is a TA for the current DMS cohort, a co-host for the DMS Rope Social and the DMS Introductory Rope Classes, and hosts Hitchin’ Bitches SoCal. She continues her rope education by attending local rope classes and intensives. Using her experiences as a top and bottom, she aims to educate using both perspectives, emphasizing personal responsibility and consent.

KALUTIKA

Topics of previous classes and demos include flogging and cigar play. He is also becoming known for his class "Chemical Warfare: Weaponized Affection" which covers use of different essential oils (capsaicin, menthol, etc.) in kink play.

MASTER FEENIX

Feenix, a professional rigger and photographer for adult entertainment, teaches locally in Los Angeles on a variety of subjects including D/s dynamics and sensual domination.

ñYADOM

ñyaDom became fascinated with rope and knots as a Boy Scout. This propelled his desire to learn Kinbaku.... that and... well you know! ;)
He joined DMS as a student in the 4th Cell cohort, graduating in 2016. He has since given back to the community as a TA for the subsequent DMS cohorts and co-host for the DMS monthly rope Social and Introductory Classes. He is presently hosting those two monthly events. He has also co-hosted the rope taster booths at Threshold Clubhouse in Los Angeles. He has continued his rope education by attending local weekend intensives with Nawashi Kanna, Pedro, Wykd Dave, Osada Steve, Kazami Ranki, Otonawa and others. 2018 saw him journey outside the LA rope scene attending Bondage Expo Dallas and Folsom Street Fair.

OJIPAN and GOODMOSTTIMES

The primary instructors behind the current Devil Mask Society Cell classes, Ojipan and goodmosttimes generally present together but can teach separately as requested. As a teaching team of switches, they specialize in addressing topics from both sides of a tie. Classes generally fall in the beginner to intermediate skill level range. They tailor the curriculum to the specifications of the host. They have travelled to teach intensives and at conventions throughout the US and to Canada. View their class list.

PAPERD0LL

Paperd0ll is a graduate of Devil Mask Society’s 6th Cell Cohort where she proudly earned the nickname, “Adult Panda.” She first entered the fetish scene in the mid-90’s when she frequented goth and industrial dance clubs and spent most of her time (and money) going to Brit-pop concerts. She began dabbling in rope in mid-2014, and strives to continually improve. She is a graduate of National Holistic Institute Los Angeles, and often incorporates her anatomy and massage training into her ropework. Although she is an avid rope enthusiast, she's a busy & multidimensional girl with many interests that she never has enough hours in the day to explore.

RHAPSODIC

Rhapsodic is a rope instructor based in Long Beach, CA. A graduate of 4th Cell and Teaching Assistant for 5th and 6th Cells, they are always looking to hone their skills as both a teacher and rigger. They enjoy teaching female and non-binary riggers, people who tie bottoms who are bigger than them, butts, and encouraging representation of the queer community in rope.
