ALTBRONTE

AltBronte has been doing rope in some fashion since 2012. She tops, bottoms, and self-suspends, and is happy to talk or teach about any of those subjects. Her style is semi-sadistic, playful, and constantly evolving. She offers classes about non-TK chest harnesses, tying as a smaller top, rope handling for short people, self-suspension, and intermediate bondage for sex, and is always developing new things to teach.

DEMONSIX

Demonsix is a student of historical kinbaku and specializes in connecting the dots from then to now. Some of his previous classes include Kinbaku 360 (with Faviola Llervu and other notable guests) and Box Tie Backtrack (with Kikorope) at Bondage Expo Dallas 2015.

KALUTIKA

Topics of previous classes and demos include flogging and cigar play. He is also becoming known for his class "Chemical Warfare: Weaponized Affection" which covers use of different essential oils (capsaicin, menthol, etc.) in kink play.

MASTER FEENIX

Feenix, a professional rigger and photographer for adult entertainment, teaches locally in Los Angeles on a variety of subjects including D/s dynamics and sensual domination.

MISTERBACON

MisterBacon teaches monthly introductory rope classes at The Attic in Culver City. With his rope partner and co-teacher, champagnerain, he also teaches "Rope Adaptation: Getting the Scene You Want" and "How To Tie Like A Jerk". He is available for private instruction in Los Angeles, and he is open to teaching around the US as requested. Classes are generally appropriate for all skill levels, but he is also happy to cover more advanced topics. He and champagnerain have taught classes regionally in LA, Santa Barbara, and San Diego, CA.

OJIPAN and GOODMOSTTIMES

The primary instructors behind the current Devil Mask Society Cell classes, Ojipan and Goodmosttimes generally present together but can teach separately as requested. As a teaching team of switches, they specialize in addressing topics from both sides of a tie. Classes generally fall in the beginner to intermediate skill level range. They tailor the curriculum to the specifications of the host. They have travelled to teach intensives and at conventions throughout the US and to Canada. View their class list.

RHAPSODIC

Rhapsodic is a rope instructor based in Long Beach, CA. A graduate of 4th Cell and Teaching Assistant for 5th and 6th Cells, she is always looking to hone her skills as both a teacher and rigger. She enjoys teaching other female riggers, people who tie bottoms who are bigger than them, butts, and encouraging representation of the queer community in rope.
