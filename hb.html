What is Hitchin' Bitches SoCal?

Hitchin' Bitches SoCal is a division of Hitchin' Bitches International, founded by Hedwig Florence.

Hitchin' Bitches is an international network of rope tops / dominants / switches who identify as women, transgender, nonbinary, genderqueer, and intersexed persons who feel that they still have links to women’s communities.

Hitchin' Bitches SoCal hosts a monthly volunteer-led event for female-identified riggers, rope tops, and switches to get together and learn rope bondage. We practice peer-to-peer learning concepts. We believe that everyone has something they can learn from others.

All rope bottoms are welcome.

Pop culture tends to portray rope bondage using stereotypical "one size fits all" imagery that doesn't reflect the reality in which we live. Rope bondage is for everybody and every body.

While our group is volunteer-led, our volunteers strive to conduct themselves according to the Devil Mask Society’s Cell Class Expectations (specifically those around Privacy, Timeliness, and Preparedness) as well as certain practices of KECC.

Our event is moderated by HitchinBitchesLA.

Our volunteers are affiliated Cell graduates of The Devil Mask Society.

You are welcome provided that you read and follow our rules.

About Our Events

While our group is centered around "Women Who Tie," we leave it to our members to decide if that is congruent with their personal rope identity. We believe that gender is on a spectrum. We don't judge books by their covers, and we are NOT the gender police!

While you are welcome to share your rope bondage and kink experiences within our space, Hitchin' Bitches reminds you that we all came for the rope bondage. Our space is for introverts and extroverts alike, so please keep it chill. Leave your soapboxes at home.

Our meetups occur every 4th Sunday. Our event is Public and currently held at the Threshold Clubhouse in North Hollywood.

If you are new and interested in joining our meetup for the first time, you can always message HitchinBitchesLA on Fetlife.
