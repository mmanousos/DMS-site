What is Hitchin' Bitches SoCal?


Hitchin' Bitches SoCal is a division of Hitchin' Bitches International, founded by Hedwig Florence.


It is a group for female riggers/rope tops/dominants in Southern California (Los Angeles, San Diego, and Orange County). We meet once a month to learn, share and experience rope first hand, based upon the Peer to Peer and unconference learning and sharing concepts.


We started in late 2011 and seek to provide a relaxed space in which women feel confident to start exploring ropes, as well as function as a network for those passionate about rope.


We usually meet in Downtown Los Angeles on the 2nd Sunday of the month, 13:00- 15:30, but keep track of event listings just to be safe (see calendar below). This monthly Open Tie is currently run by goodmosttimes, AltBronte, and Rhapsodic. Feel free to message one of us if you're interested in attending.


We welcome all women who live full time as women, and FTM, genderqueer and intersexed persons who feel that they have strong links to women's communities.


Please respect this group is not for male rope tops.


You can always bring a rope bottom, of any gender identity, as long as this person understands and respects the ethos of the space.


Hitchin' Bitches is first and foremost a learning environment, not a cruising spot.
