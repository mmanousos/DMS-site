What is Hitchin' Bitches SoCal?


Hitchin' Bitches SoCal is a division of Hitchin' Bitches International, founded by Hedwig Florence.

Started in 2011, HB SoCal is a rope education and practice group dedicated to empowering women, non-binary, and trans-masculine folks in Southern California (Los Angeles, San Diego, and Orange County) to improve and develop their rope skills. Our group is led by and for female, transgender, cisgender, bigender, agender, non-conforming, non-binary, two spirit, and / or genderqueer identified folks. Many play and learning spaces are centered around the cis male hetero experience, and while we do not seek to diminish these spaces, we hope to create an additional space in which tops and bottoms who feel less comfortable in those spaces can practice and enjoy rope for themselves.

We seek to make this learning space as welcoming as possible to those who have historically felt under-represented as students and as rope tops within rope communities.

If you aren’t sure if this group is for you, please feel free to message the owners.

We meet once a month to learn, share, and experience rope first hand, often using Peer to Peer and unconference learning and sharing concepts.

We usually meet in Van Nuys on the 4th Sunday of the month, 13:30-16:30, but keep track of event listings just to be safe (see calendar below). This monthly Open Tie is currently run by paperd0ll and Femininja. You must contact one of them if you are interested in attending.

You can always bring a rope bottom, of any gender identity, as long as this person understands and respects the ethos of the space.

Please respect this group is not for male rope tops.

Hitchin' Bitches is first and foremost a learning environment, not a cruising spot.
