ONE-TIME EVENTS

An Evening with Cynna Rae - July 12, 2019

Cynna, a charismatic rope slut from Austin, Texas, joining us for an evening in Van Nuys to instruct a rope lesson focusing on learning adaptable patterns and utilizing those patterns in connective play. This evening, she will lead students through a pattern for a chest and hip harness and demonstrate their use in play. Her teaching will focus on altering ties to suit the bottom's needs - including adaptations for mobility issues, physical preferences, and tying big bodies. *This is an upper-beginner level class.* Tickets available now.

A Weekend at The Bank with Cynna Rae - July 13-14, 2019

Cynna, a charismatic rope slut from Austin, Texas, joins DMS in DTLA for an *intermediate-level intensive* for those working on suspension and transition sequences. This weekend, Cynna will lead attendees through several patterns for chest and hip harnesses and demonstrate their use in intermediate-level transition sequences. Her teaching will focus on adapting ties to suit the bottoms needs - including adaptations for mobility issues, physical preferences, and tying big bodies. There will be options presented that work for ground play, partial, and full suspension. Students will be given time on both days to lab and experiment with things they have learned in class. Buy your tickets now!

Fuoco Intensive - September 21-22, 2019

DMS is thrilled to host an encore visit from Fuoco, a bad-ass rope person. She has amazing flexibility and a dynamisim while bottoming in rope that is a delight to see. She is also a contortionist and aerialist with an in-depth understanding of the human body and a rope top which allows her to assess structures and positions from both sides of the tie offering a uniquely insightful perspective. Curriculum for this weekend is TBD but will likely involve building rope bottoming skills in suspension, flexibility, and body awareness, as well as assessing ties for sustainability. Mark your calendar now - you won't want to miss this weekend!

Ongoing events occur monthly, but are subject to scheduling changes. Please check the calendar below to confirm.

DMS Rope Social

A chance to meet fellow rope enthusiasts in a low pressure environment.

DMS Introductory Rope Class

A four-month iterative series of intro level rope lessons in Japanese-style rope bondage.

Hosted by ñyaDom.

Every 4th Thursday of the month, 8 PM - 11 PM.

TieHaus Beginner Workshops and Rope Social

TieHaus offers a series of intro level rope lessons in Japanese-style rope bondage.
