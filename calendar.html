ONE-TIME EVENTS

KITTENS WITH STRING: Drills for Rope Handling and Efficiency with goodmosttimes and Ojipan - JANUARY 25, 2018

Patterns in rope are beautiful and are the main things taught to beginners so they have a benchmark to show progress. But patterns can also be time consuming and tedious to complete leaving both partners bored and frustrated. In this 80 minute class you’ll learn some tips and techniques for moving your rope fluidly and efficiently. Walk away with several new methods for practicing your new handling skills. And bottoms, your participation and feedback is vital! RSVP on Facebook or Fetlife.
